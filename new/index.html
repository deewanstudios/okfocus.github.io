Cutting Edge Technologies – Culturally Conscious Design.

OKFocus is a forward thinking, technology driven agency based in New York City. We implement cutting edge technologies in culturally conscious ways. As children of the information age, we understand the deep impact genre defining internet products can have – creating such a thing is our paramount goal. We have worked with companies such as Google, Smirnoff and Flamingo, cultural institutions such as MOCA, New Museum and The MET and artists such as Tanlines, Nicola Formichetti and MIA – realizing our forward thinking concepts with cutting edge code. We are artists, designers and programmers with the ability to see advanced projects through each stage of development. As a young company, founded in early 2012, we are optimistic for the future and driven by innovation.
