  • OKShadow

    If you are like us, you’ve seen drop shadow used on the internets. But like honestly, HOW IS THE SHADOW CAST??? Yeah, we didn’t know either. So we made OKShadow – a jQuery plugin that lets you pretend that your mouse is the sun, casting a shadow based on your mouse movement. We recently updated it to work with iOS! Now you can just rotate your iPhone or iPad around in a circle and see the SHADOW BE CAST BY THE STRENGTH OF YOUR HAND!

  • OKHover

    I don’t know about you, but like for us just underlining text on hover or whatever is boorrrrrrriiinnnnggggg! So we made OKHover, a jQuery plugin that lets you easily tile any image you want all over a website based on an element hovered upon.
