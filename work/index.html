SELECTED WORK

  • Draw With Your Face

    Commissioned by Google, Draw With Your Face is a Google Hangout experiment that pushes the limits of technology and fun. Making use of face-tracking, Draw With Your Face allows users to do just that, the result is a fun, funny and fast paced game.

  • Dance Exchange

    One night, twelve parties, hundreds of animated gifs, and thousands of unique visitors. Blending tech and nightlife – we put the party online.

  • Tug of Store

    Created for the debut of Svpply's API, Tug of Store allows users to rate products together in real-time. Called "the zeitgeist of 2012" by Wolff Olins, Tug of Store pushed the limits of interaction online.

  • WhoDat.biz

    The "Facebook of Websites" – 100,000+ hits in one day. What other digital studio do you know that got a cease and desist from Kanye West?

  • Ends of the Earth

    Created for The Museum of Contemporary Art, the Ends of the Earth website makes use of Google Maps API allowing users to explore the greatest land art found all over the globe.
