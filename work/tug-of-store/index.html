Svpply - Tug of Store

Tug of War

Svpply is a service that helps you find the products you love, from the people and stores you find interesting. When they approached us to create a web-app to draw some attention to release their API, we were thrilled. With our early, un-restricted access to their API we decided to rethink the classic "hot or not" paradigm, except with a tactile real world analogy – tug of war.

Cool or Crap? Let “Clique Democracy” Decide.

Even though there is a beautiful image from Svpply's database on the page at all times, the first thing most people notice on Tug of Store are the other cursors. Those cursors are *real people* voting whether a product is "cool" or "crap" at the same time as you are. This was a large technical challenge, but totally worth it – new interaction is important to us as it promotes a feeling of instantaneous oneness.

Not All Fun and Games

While Tug of Store might seem like it was all fun and games (almost too much fun), some interesting data came out of it. Svpply got to get a pretty good sentiment analysis of various items and stores from their database. While the surveying method might not have been all that scientific, it sure did turn some heads.
