MOCA - Ends of the Earth

Globally Centric, Exhibition Specific

Created for the Geffen Contemporary at MOCA, Ends of Earth: Land Art to 1974 is the first large-scale, historical-thematic exhibition to deal broadly with Land Art. We were glad to lend our experience working with artists and museums to create an immersive exhibition-specific website for MOCA.

10,000 Foot View

When this project was just a kernel of an idea, it was very important to us that as many people as possible would be able to use this website. This distinction led us to choose Google Maps over similar products like Google Earth. By leveraging the latest in Google Maps' API, we were able to deliver a cross-browser, cross-device art viewing experience like no other.
