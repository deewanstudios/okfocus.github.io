Side Projects

Side projects are very important to us as a company. Having self-initiated projects is what keeps our work top notch and lets us experiment with new technologies and ideas to proliferate throughout the internet.

The Facebook of Websites

Being mindful of culture is paramount to design. Context is key, so when we thought up the ridiculous idea of a faux Kanye West branded startup centered around the age old internet function of WhoIs, we knew exactly what we were doing. Shortly after launching, false reporting ran the gamut from Betabeat to the Washington Post. The project is frequently cited by reporters as one of the largest "internet hoaxes" as of late and was featured in the BBC, LA Times and even managed to appear on the front page of The Wall Street Journal.
