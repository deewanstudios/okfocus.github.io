The policy accounts for accessories such as plain stockings or tights in neutral, white or navy blue are permissible. Leggings that do not cover the entire leg and foot are not permissible. Students will not be allowed to wear hats of any kind with the exception of ones used for religious reasons. Students will not be allowed to wear sunglasses and midriffs must be covered at all times.

Shorts must be khaki or navy blue. Cargo shorts will not be allowed. Additionally, shorts must sit at knee length.

The Saddle Brook School District is considering school uniforms.

The possibility of mandatory school uniforms for students is being raised due to concerns by members of the school board and district concerning students paying attention during class. In a press release, the SBBOE stated, "the staff at Saddle Brook Schools take seriously the responsibility to educate students and prepare them to meet life goals. This cannot be done effectively when inappropriate clothing creates a disruption to the educational process. Specific standards are those of decency, safety, good grooming and non-interference with teaching and learning."

Lastly according to the press release, "school administrators have the right to determine what is appropriate," or conversely, not appropriate.
